üåç Introduction

Water is one of the most essential natural resources, yet its availability is increasingly under threat due to rising global demand, climate change, and inefficient usage. Proper water management is crucial to ensure sustainability, reduce wastage, and optimize consumption.

The IoT-Based Water Monitoring and Management System aims to provide an efficient solution for tracking and managing water usage in real-time. By integrating Raspberry Pi 4 with water flow sensors, this system continuously monitors water consumption, detects leaks, and sends alerts when abnormal usage patterns are detected.

Additionally, the system enables administrators to set usage thresholds and implement dynamic tariff structures, encouraging responsible water consumption.
